What We Offer

Building Strong Foundations for Your Child

Play-Based Learning & Game

We follow a play-based curriculum where children learn numbers, letters, and basic concepts through games, puzzles, and hands-on activities. This helps improve memory, focus, and problem-solving skills naturally.

Creative Activities

Daily activities like drawing, coloring, music, dance, and storytelling help children express their thoughts and emotions. These activities also improve creativity, imagination, and fine motor skills.

Caring & Trained Teachers

Our teachers are trained in early childhood education and understand how to handle kids with patience and care. Each child gets personal attention to support their learning pace and emotional needs.

Healthy Mind & Habits

We teach children good habits like cleanliness, sharing, discipline, and basic routines. Along with this, we focus on emotional development so children feel confident, secure, and happy.

Facilities

Safe, smart & well-equipped facilities for kids

#1
Pick-up & Drop Service

We provide safe and reliable transport for children at affordable rates. Our drivers are well-trained and drive very carefully, ensuring your child’s daily travel is smooth and secure. Parents can either use this service or manage pick-up and drop on their own—this facility is completely optional.

#2
Morning Assembly with Sound System

The daily assembly is conducted using a sound system and speakers, so every child can hear clearly. The day begins with the national anthem, promoting respect & unity. We follow a two-uniform system during the week, building discipline and a sense of belonging.

#3
CCTV Surveillance

Our entire campus is monitored with CCTV cameras to ensure complete safety.
Trained staff members are always present to keep a close watch on children throughout the day. We also follow strict safety protocols to maintain a secure and caring environment.

#4
Fully Secured Campus

The school premises are fully secured with proper entry control and child-safe infrastructure to prevent any unwanted access.

#5
Caring Support Staff (Dai Care)

Our support staff takes care of children with patience and attention, helping them feel comfortable, safe, and well looked after throughout the day..

#6
Computer Classes

Basic computer exposure and smart learning tools are introduced in a simple and fun way to build early digital awareness.
